On The Line: How Schooling, Housing, and Civil Rights Shaped Hartford and its Suburbs

Chapter 8 Teaching with On The Line

This chapter describes how the book emerged through working with liberal arts undergraduates and Hartford-area community partners in the Cities Suburbs and Schools seminar at Trinity College. In addition, it provides educators with lesson ideas to help students of all ages explore key questions about past and present source materials.
